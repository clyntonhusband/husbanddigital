Simple, Secure Storage

Storage Liskeard provides straightforward, no-nonsense storage solutions for local residents and businesses. Located just off the A390, we offer easy access without the hassle of navigating town centre traffic.

Our secure, gated facility features 20-foot steel shipping containers for rent on flexible month-to-month contracts. We also provide outdoor storage space for boats, caravans, motorhomes, and cars. With 24/7 access, CCTV monitoring, and you keeping the only key to your unit, your belongings stay secure and accessible whenever you need them.
